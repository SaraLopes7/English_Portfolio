Who is Sara Lopes and what she is doing

August 08, 2003 Sara Lopes

Hey guys! My name is Sara Lopes, I'm 20 years old and I'm studying systems analysis and development at Senac Pernambuco College.

Hey guys! My name is Sara Lopes, I'm 20 years old and I do a faculty of analysis and development of systems at Senac Pernambuco College, through the Embarque Digital.

Currently, I work at Neurotech, one of Porto Digital's startups. There I am as an intern at Squad Seguros, one of the Squads of the operations area, where I develop policies, focus on inquiries and be part of the support team for all the clients of our Squad who are the car insurers.

I'm the kind of person who can never sit still, so in addition to going to college in the morning and internship in the afternoon, I'm always taking courses in the evening! I recently did one on AWS (Amazon Web Services) tools and am currently studying networking and containerization with platforms like Azure and Google Cloud.

I love programming with FrontEnd but my real beach is the backEnd with languages like Python, the sadness of Java and NodeJs.

My hobbies include reading books and manga, watching series, movies, and anime, and playing digital games. Being a person who is never stopped, I am very tired so the whole time you will see me yawning and dying of sleep because I will probably come at night doing some code or activity, like now that it is 2:26 am on a weekday and I have to "wake up" at 6 am to go to class.

My main career and personal focus lately has been to work on my interpersonal communication as I have always been an EXTREMELY shy person.

Well, basically, that's me.
